About A-very
Special Treat
A-very Special Story was created by a loving parent who named the pastry shop after their daughter, Avery, whose bright spirit sparked the inspiration behind every recipe on the menu. Now, the shop honors those cherished moments by serving pastries crafted with the same warmth, creativity, and sweetness Avery shared with everyone around her.
