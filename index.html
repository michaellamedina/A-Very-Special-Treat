How it all began... A-very Special Story

A-Very Special Story began when a devoted parent named their pastry shop after their daughter, Avery, whose joyful presence inspired every recipe that now fills the shop. Today, A-Very Special Story stands as a heartfelt reminder of those memories, offering pastries made with the same care, inspiration, and sweetness Avery brought into their lives.

Made special just for you! A-very Special Season

A-Very Special Season highlights our pastry shop’s dedication to making every celebration unforgettable by offering custom catering for your most meaningful moments. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a grand event, we craft each special order with care, creativity, and the signature sweetness our shop is known for. From elegant cakes to beautifully arranged pastry selections, we tailor every detail to match your theme, flavor preferences, and vision.
